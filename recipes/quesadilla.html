Quesadilla

Description

A quesadilla is a Mexican dish consisting of a tortilla that is filled primarily with cheese, and sometimes meats, spices, and other fillings, and then cooked on a griddle or stove.
Traditionally, a corn tortilla is used, but it can also be made with a flour tortilla.

Ingredients

  • large flour tortillas
  • grated cheese
  • olive oil / butter

Steps

  1. Heat the tortillas until air pockets form
  2. Add the cheese and other ingredients
  3. Lower the heat and cover pan
  4. Fold the tortilla over
  5. Remove quesadilla from pan and cut into wedges
